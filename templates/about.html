About handsome.ly

What is this service?

We're here to make the experience of getting your haircut the best it can be.

We know that your time is valuable and we’re trying to get salons and barbers to better appreciate that.

We're just getting started on our mission. We're focused on Cambridge at the moment while we grow - cus that's where we live and we know how busy people can be here.

No queues
Your barber can tell you when they’re free, so you know there won’t be a long queue when you get there.
Discounts
If you can squeeze in at short notice, or fill a cancelled appointment you are doing the salon a favor. They may encourage this behavious by offering a discount.
Customer choice
We’re going to show opening times, prices and reviews of all the places you can get your hair cut. You can make an informed choice of what’s best for you.
And completely free
The service has a lovely price of £0.00

How to use

  1. Select salons on the map that you'd like to get a haircut with
  2. We'll then email you anytime they're free and any discount they're offering
  3. You can then:
    • Accept = You pop over and get a great haircut
    • Decline = You can't make this one but you still want to get notified about future oportunities
    • Cancel notification
