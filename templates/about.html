So, what is handsome.ly?

Handsome.ly allows your favourite hair salons in Cambridge to offer out reduced-price haircuts at times when they otherwise wouldn't have any customers.

This means that you get a stylish haircut for less dolla, and gives salons the opportunity to maximise their capacity. Everyone wins!


How to use

Simple!

  1. Select the type of haircut you would like
  2. Choose from a salon and the discount they offer
  3. We'll then email you when they have a free appointment and the discount they're offering
  4. You can then:
    • Accept = You pop over and get a great haircut
    • Decline = You can't make this one but you still want to get notified about future opportunities
    • Cancel notifications
