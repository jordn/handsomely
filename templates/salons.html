Why you should use handsome.ly at your salon

Handsome.ly is a service to bring you more customers at times that are convenient to you. We're tying together the needs of customers and salons to make a service that is valuable to both. A customer applies to your salon and you tell them when is a good time to come.

Great for salons

Fewer, empty timeslots
An empty timeslot is a lost opportunity for revenue. You can control when a customer comes in and fill up those last minute cancelled appointments
Balance customers throughout the day
Customers can be directed to the times that are otherwise quiet for you, and you can encourage customers by offering a discount
New customers
Your salon will show up in online results, exposing it to new customers
Doesn't take time away from your work
We've made using the system as simple as possible so you can spend your time doing what you do best (...cutting hair)

All salons and barbershops suffer from the same problem. There are times in the day that are less desirable for customers to have a haircut. Equally, there are other times that are so popular you have to bring in more staff or turn down people's requests.

A partial solution has been to offer people who are price-critical but fairly flexible on time (typically students) a discount for coming in on the quieter midweek days.

However, the salon still has little control over when people are likely to come in, and the demand can still change significantly throughout the day.

Handsome.ly is a waitlist system for people that are interested in getting a haircut with your salon soon. With a simple click of a button on a computer or phone, you can bring customers to you when it's quiet, or offer out a cancelled appointment to people looking to get their hair cut with you as soon as possible.


Handsome.ly leads to happier customers too.

They can select your salon on their phone where ever they may be, and be notified instantly with your communication.
